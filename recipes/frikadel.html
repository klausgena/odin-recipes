Frikadel Recipe

Description

For this recipe, you need a bike and you need to be quick. Nobody wants a cold frikadel on his (or her) plate! This delicious piece of processed meat and other mystery ingredients needs to burn your tongue to the third degree!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Plasma
  • To taste: ketchup (tomatoe or curry), chopped onion, mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Grab the keys to your house
  2. Get out and close the door behind you. You don't want to get robbed!
  3. Hop on your bike and drive to the frituur on the bridge
  4. Get in line and order a frikadel. If you're celebrating an important date, make it a frikadel speciaal.
  5. Wait for 5 minutes. In the meantime, get up to speed with the latest intrigues of episode 2109 of Flemish soap series Familie.
  6. Take the plastic bag, go home! Drive quickly, because hot is the message!
Go home.
