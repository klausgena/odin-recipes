Kapsalon Recipe

Description

A tasty and healthy, protein packed, evening dish for the entire family. Feeds six! Leave some for the dog? Don't think so!

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Healthy probiotics
  • Melted cheese
  • Softened fries

Steps

  1. Grab the keys to your house
  2. Get out and close the door behind you. You don't want to get robbed!
  3. Go to the Turkish doner place on the corner of your street
  4. Get in line and order a kapsalon
  5. Wait for 45 minutes. In the meantime, do some doom scrolling and drink a fresh plastic cup of delicious salty ayran.
  6. Take the plastic bag, go home and have a feast!
Go home.
