Lasagne Recipe

Description

Sometimes you feel a craving for the tangy taste of aluminium foil on the side your tong. Eating supermarket lasagna is what the doctor ordered! Beats licking batteries!

Ingredients

  • Aluminium foil
  • Tomato sauce with soap
  • Meat like substance

Steps

  1. Check the freezer. You knew it! In the bottom drawer, under the spinach and the repurposed ice cream bucket with the unknown substance, a lasagna is winking at you.
  2. Drop it in the microwave.
  3. Bad idea, it belongs in the oven! 200 degrees.
  4. Wait for 45 minutes. In the meantime, put on the telly and eat some ice cream.
Go home.
